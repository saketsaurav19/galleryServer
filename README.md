**Explore, Experience, and Evaluate the Future of On-Device Generative AI with Google AI Edge.**

**Google AI Edge Gallery** is the premier destination for running the world's most powerful open-source Large Language Models (LLMs) right on your mobile device. Experience high-performance Generative AI directly on your own hardware—fully offline, private, and lightning-fast.

### 🌟 Now Featuring: Gemma 4

Our latest version brings official support for the newly released **Gemma 4** family! As the centerpiece of this update, Gemma 4 allows you to test the cutting edge of on-device AI. Experience advanced reasoning, logic, and creative capabilities without ever sending your data to a server.

## ✨ Core Features

* 🧠 **AI Chat with Thinking Mode:** Engage in fluid, multi-turn conversations. Toggle the new *Thinking Mode* to peek "under the hood" and see the model’s step-by-step reasoning process—perfect for understanding complex problem-solving. *(Currently supports models starting with the Gemma 4 family).*
* 🛠️ **Agent Skills:** Transform your LLM from a simple conversationalist into a proactive assistant. Augment model capabilities with tools like Wikipedia for fact-grounding, interactive maps, and rich visual summary cards. Load modular skills directly from a URL or browse community contributions on GitHub Discussions.
* 👁️ **Ask Image:** Harness multimodal power! Identify objects, solve visual puzzles, or get detailed descriptions using your device’s camera or photo gallery.
* 🎙️ **Audio Scribe:** Transcribe and translate voice recordings into text in real-time, utilizing high-efficiency on-device language models.
* 🧪 **Prompt Lab:** A dedicated workspace to test different prompts and single-turn use cases with granular control over model parameters like temperature and top-k.
* ⚙️ **Mobile Actions:** Unlock offline device controls and automated tasks powered entirely by a finetune of FunctionGemma 270M.
* 🌱 **Tiny Garden:** A fun, experimental mini-game where you use natural language to plant and harvest a virtual garden using a finetune of FunctionGemma 270M.
* 📊 **Model Management & Benchmarking:** Gallery is your flexible sandbox for open-source models. Easily download from our curated list or load your own custom models. Manage your library and run benchmark tests to see exactly how each model performs on your specific hardware.
* 🔒 **100% On-Device Privacy:** All model inferences happen directly on your device. **No internet required**, ensuring total privacy for your prompts, images, and sensitive data.

## 🏁 Get Started in Minutes!

1. **Check OS Requirements**: Android 12+ or iOS 17+.
2. **Download the App**:
   - Install from [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.ai.edge.gallery) or the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/google-ai-edge-gallery/id6749645337).
   - Alternatively, grab the APK from the [**latest release**](https://github.com/google-ai-edge/gallery/releases/latest/).
3. **Install & Explore**: For detailed installation instructions (including setups for corporate devices) and a comprehensive user guide, check out our [**Project Wiki**](https://github.com/google-ai-edge/gallery/wiki)!

## 🛠️ Technology Highlights

* **Google AI Edge:** Core APIs and tools for on-device machine learning.
* **LiteRT:** Lightweight runtime for highly optimized model execution.
* **Hugging Face Integration:** Seamless model discovery and downloading.
